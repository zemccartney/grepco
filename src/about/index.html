What's with GrepCo?

grep is a classic search utility, dating back to the prehistoric days of Unix. When I learned that grep stood for "global regular expression search and print," I thought Global Regular Expressions sounded like a satirical agency name, something out of Better Off Ted, which left needing a p somewhere and I thought production company had a better ring to it than productions, along with some actual name differentiation from the program itself.
